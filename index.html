About Me

Before coming to the MSAN program, I got an MS in Chemistry from the University of Minnesota and a bachelor's in chemistry from Oberlin College. I almost double-majored in chemistry and studio art, but the lab and studio time was prohibitive. I like to knit and will do so in class if my mind is wandering off from the subject at hand, though I will put it away if I think that it is bothering or distracting someone. It does not mean that I am paying any less attention!

My main interest in analytics has to do with healthcare analytics (epidemiology is awesome!), but it is not limited to that--most any project with some sort of public works aspect to it is something I would be interested in working on. My practicum project is with the County of LA predicting potential poll workers.
