About My Graphs

Basic Functionality

My basic functionality is a simple bubble chart of life expectancy versus murder rates. I have made the bubble transparent so that smaller bubbles do not get obscured by larger bubbles. This transparency also means that the bubbles can overlap with the axes without obscuring information so I did not have to clip the bubbles at the edge of the graph. I made sure that there as enough padding around the graph that there was room for the bubble. Note that the area of the bubble corresponds to the area of the state it represents.
